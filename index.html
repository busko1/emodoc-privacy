1. Information we collect

Account information

When you sign in with Apple, Google, or email, we receive a unique identifier and, depending on the method, your email address and display name. You may also use LitVoice as a guest, in which case no account information is collected.

Content you provide

LitVoice processes text, images of book pages, and URLs that you choose to add to the app. This content is used only to generate audio playback and to populate your personal library.

Voice recordings (optional)

If you choose to use the voice cloning feature, you record a short audio sample. This sample is sent to our voice synthesis provider to create a personal voice model used exclusively for your account.

Usage and diagnostic data

We collect anonymized data about how the app is used (features opened, errors encountered, session length) to improve performance and stability. This data does not personally identify you.

Subscription data

Subscription and purchase information is handled by Apple and our subscription provider (RevenueCat). We receive your subscription status and entitlements but do not see your payment details.

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide core features: text scanning, text-to-speech synthesis, voice selection, library management.
  • Generate audio playback from the content you provide.
  • Create and manage voice clones and designed voices you opt to use.
  • Process subscriptions, credits, and entitlements.
  • Diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.
  • Respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your content to train general-purpose AI models.

3. Voice data and AI processing

On-device voices

iOS system voices run entirely on your device. No text or audio is sent to our servers when you use a system voice.

Cloud voices

When you use a premium AI narrator, voice clone, or designed voice, the text you want to synthesize is sent over a secure connection to our voice synthesis provider. The audio is generated and returned to your device. Text submissions are not retained by us beyond the time required to deliver playback.

Voice cloning

Voice samples you record for cloning are transmitted to the synthesis provider to build a voice model linked to your account. You may delete cloned voices at any time from the app, which removes the associated voice model from the provider.

Voice design

Text prompts used to design new voices are sent to the synthesis provider and used to generate a unique voice profile linked to your account.

4. Third-party services

LitVoice relies on the following trusted third-party services. Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

  • Apple Sign In - authentication.
  • Google Sign In - authentication.
  • RevenueCat - subscription management.
  • ElevenLabs - cloud voice synthesis, cloning, and design.
  • Apple App Store - in-app purchases and subscriptions.
  • Crash and analytics tools - anonymized diagnostics.

We share only the minimum data needed for each service to function.

5. Data storage and security

Your library, including scanned pages, text, and book metadata, is stored locally on your device. Voice models and account-linked data are stored on our providers' secure servers.

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS) for all data in transit and rely on our providers' encryption-at-rest for stored data. While we take reasonable precautions, no system is completely secure.

You can delete your account and associated cloud data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

6. Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Withdraw consent for processing.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at emodocinc@gmail.com.

7. Children's privacy

LitVoice is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy
